Our Story

Our obsession with love of air plants started on a trip to St. Augustine, FL when we were wedding venue hunting. We stumbled upon some air plants in a shop, and were immediately taken aback by their unique beauty, and the endless decorating possibilities that came along with them. We decided to create a business that would allow our customers easy access to these exotic yet hearty plants, and one that reflected our own personal style and design.
