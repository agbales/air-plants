Terrariums
Plant homes!

Our Simplicity Air Plant Terrariums are designed to showcase the modern beauty of our air plants. This hanging glass terrarium features whimsical Xerographica air plant. The Xerographica is the mini version, and will continue to grow for many years and get much larger. This type of tillandsia requires very minimal maintenance and enjoys misting and dunking rather than being soaked. The Xerographica air plant is a desert region plant, and one of few varieties that can handle full sun.
